Division of Psychology and Language Sciences, University College London

Research

I am currently studying for a PhD at University College London on a 4-year Biomedical Sciences program funded by the Medical Research Council.

My research aims to investigate the behavioural and neural mechanisms of empathy through the study of typical and atypical populations. I am also interested in social information processing more broadly, and in disorders that are characterised by atypical social information processing, such as psychopathy and autism spectrum disorders.

I have used behavioural, psychopharmacological, brain stimulation and neuroimaging techniques to investigate these questions.
